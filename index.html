Sellers Overview

A straightforward approach to premium property exits

Our Philosophy

No deal closes unless a seller feels like they are winning.

Stoneholdt Partners is an acquisition company operating cleanly within the residential redevelopment space. Our job is simple: we thoroughly understand exactly what you are looking for out of an exit—timeline, terms, and final valuation expectations—and construct a transparent roadmap to perform on.

The Transaction Blueprint

Skip the hiring process, the public open houses, the structural repair negotiations, and the standard agent commission fees. We protect your transaction from standard retail fallouts by mapping out clear, predictable baseline expectations before we ever put pen to paper.

STAGE 01

Under Contract

Once we come to an agreement on terms and completely align expectations for the transaction, we finalize our contract structure and cleanly open escrow.

STAGE 02

Feasibility Period

A structured 30-day window for our development partners to complete preliminary surveys and soil samples to formally clear structural and engineering boundaries.

STAGE 03

Earnest Money

Upon successfully clearing the standard base milestones, non-refundable earnest money deposits are released to secure our full skin in the game.

STAGE 04

Close & Record

Title transfers safely through escrow, funds register instantly via direct wire, and your custom post-closing move-out arrangements activate flawlessly.
